# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want `pkg-ct` to be a practical, welcoming project for people improving
JavaScript and TypeScript dependency health.

Participants are expected to be respectful, constructive, and patient.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and specific.
- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence.
- Keep feedback focused on the code, documentation, or behavior.
- Help newcomers understand the project before asking for large changes.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information.
- Dismissive or insulting comments.
- Spam or repeated off-topic promotion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; or block users
when needed to keep the project safe and useful.
