INDUSTRY RESEARCH TO FOLLOW

Use these principles throughout implementation:

Peer Dependency Research

Peer dependency conflicts are complex and often involve optional peers, alternative peers, and ecosystem-specific requirements. Not every missing peer should be treated as a hard conflict.

Production Dependency Research

Research shows that not all runtime dependencies are truly production-relevant and not all development dependencies stay out of production. Production classification must be evidence-based rather than simplistic.

SemVer Research

Patch and minor version duplication is common in npm ecosystems and should not automatically be treated as a serious problem. Severity should scale with version distance and upgrade impact.
